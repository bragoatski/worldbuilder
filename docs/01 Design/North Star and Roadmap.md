## The three known problems (status 2026-06-23)
1. **Ecosystem balance (the heart of the game). DONE + SHIPPED 2026-06-23.** Fixed by a measured knob loop, not hand-tuned blind. Outcome "C2" (knobs A+D+C): on the 1000-tick protocol, full-extinction 17%->0%, carnivore-persistence 0%->83%, phase lag -44t->+68t. Spatial dispersion (herbivore conspecific crowding) was the load-bearing lever. The remaining long-run instability is the PARADOX OF ENRICHMENT under a non-stationary world (carrying capacity K rises without bound as terrain grows -> cycle amplitude grows -> some seeds crash); accepted as organic living-world behavior. Full record: `Ecosystem Tuning Log.md`. The steady-state long-run fix is deferred -> Future projects below.
2. **Rivers. ACTIVE - next up.** Two prior bespoke attempts (v1: full water tiles, too much board became water; v2: curved overlay flowing downhill). The real problem: the tracer is bolted onto terrain with no coherent drainage, so clean rivers rarely generate and the feature cannot even be evaluated. The fix is the standard method: depression-fill (priority-flood) -> flow direction -> flow accumulation -> threshold for width. This guarantees dendritic rivers by construction. See Balance Proposal Item 2.
3. **Beaches. SLATED FOR REMOVAL (Kevin, 2026-06-23)** -> Future projects below. A uniform yellow edge appearing everywhere at once; fundamentally a sub-tile rendering problem at 6px resolution. Cut it (remove `beachStep` from the tick + the beach branch of `draw` + the erosion coupling).

## Future projects (backlog - Kevin 2026-06-23, NOT current work)
Deferred deliberately so rivers stays the focus. Each is a self-contained future unit:
1. **Steady-state long-run ecosystem balance.** C2 is window-balanced but not steady-state because terrain genesis never stops, so the prey carrying capacity K rises without bound (paradox of enrichment - see Engineering Lessons). Rate knobs CANNOT reach this (proven: local density-dependent birth is inert under the dispersion knob; more dispersion backfires by raising effective K). The two real fixes: (a) BOUND K generation-side - cap land/flora growth so the world settles at a target development level, then the existing C2 knobs give genuine steady-state balance (cleanest root fix; touches terrain generation; needs a call on the target land/flora level); or (b) add an ENRICHMENT-ROBUST mechanism - predator interference (carnivores hunt less efficiently when crowded, symmetric to the herbivore crowding knob) or a prey predation-refuge. Start with (a). `faunaMaxPop` is currently kept at 400 as a benign backstop.
2. **Remove beaches.** Cut the beach system entirely: remove `beachStep` from the tick pipeline, the beach branch of `draw`, the beachLevel erosion->ocean coupling, and the beach toolbar/legend bits. The sim loses nothing functional. Lowest-risk cleanup; reclaims complexity.
3. **Improve creature AI.** The fauna foraging / predator-avoidance behavior lives in `scoreTileForFauna` (movement scoring) + `faunaStep` (eat / reproduce / energy). Current behavior is greedy local tile-scoring. Room to make it richer / smarter (better foraging, herd/pack behavior, predator pursuit, prey evasion, memory) - scope to be defined when picked up. Keep it MEASURED with the harness so behavior changes do not silently break the now-balanced ecosystem.
